Kuruier – Privacy Policy

  • 1. Information We Collect
    Personal details: name, phone, email, ID documents (for KYC).
    Shipment details: sender & receiver info, package type, and value.
    Payment details: payment method, transaction records.
    Device data: IP, location, usage analytics for security and performance.
  • 2. How We Use Data
    To verify identities (Senders, Travelers).
    To process shipments, payments, and refunds.
    To prevent fraud and ensure compliance with law.
    To improve user experience and provide customer support.
  • 3. Data Sharing
    With payment partners for processing transactions.
    With KYC service providers for verification.
    With authorities if required by law.
    Never sold to third parties for marketing.
  • 4. Data Security
    All transactions use encrypted payment gateways.
    KYC and ID data are stored securely and accessed only for compliance.
  • 5. Data Retention
    Retained as long as your account is active or as required by law.
    Users may request account deletion by contacting support.
  • 6. User Rights
    Request access to your data.
    Update or correct personal details.
    Request deletion of your account (subject to compliance obligations).
  • 7. Cookies
    Kuruier uses cookies for session management and analytics.
  • 8. Contact
    Privacy Queries: privacy@kuruier.com
